"Experience"


University of Maryland Department of IT
Software Engineer Intern
Feb. 2022 - Present
During my tenure as a member of the UMD DIT team, I spent time working on our Integrated Operations Center (IOC) which handles the monitoring of many services used on campus and Identity and Access Management (IAM) which manages the campus userbase for online web resources. Our tech stack was mostly serverless and included AWS Lambda, AWS DynamoDB, NodeJS, and VueJS.

Here are some of my notable roles and responsiblities in this position:

  • Aided deployment of IOC Bot by resolving feature and issues on Jira tickets and documenting the functionality of the bot on Confluence
  • Started development of a testing harness to prevent any downtime and misconfiguration issues related to school's Shibboleth setup for user management.
  • Worked on finishing the development of IAM's TestUserBuilder feature which allows for CRUD operations on mock LDAP users used in our testing protocols for a variety of services.
